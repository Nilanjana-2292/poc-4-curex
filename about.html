About Us

At Curex, we believe healthcare should be simple, accessible, and personal.

Our experienced general physicians provide quality medical care for individuals and families — from routine checkups to urgent consultations.

Whether it’s a seasonal illness, preventive advice, or family wellness, Curex is your trusted partner in everyday health. With compassionate service and 24×7 availability, we’re here to care for you anytime, anywhere.

Whether you're dealing with a seasonal illness, need preventive health advice, or are managing a chronic condition, Curex provides expert guidance and timely treatment. Our services also extend to male and female wellness checks, and pediatric care — ensuring your whole family is covered under one roof.

We operate 24×7 to ensure that no health concern goes unanswered. From online consultations to in-person visits, we make sure quality care is never out of reach.

At Curex, your well-being is our mission.

We’re here for you — every hour, every day.
